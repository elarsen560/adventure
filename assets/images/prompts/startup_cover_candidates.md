# Startup Cover Candidates

Use these prompts to generate the first startup-screen image candidates for Asterfall Observatory.

The goal is to find the visual house style before generating room plates.

## Shared Style Block

Apply this style direction to every candidate:

- retro early-1990s DOS VGA adventure-game background art
- hand-painted pixel-art scene
- 320x200 composition logic
- visible pixel structure
- restrained 256-color feel
- moody but readable
- storm-dark coastal atmosphere
- no text
- no logo
- no watermark
- no characters unless explicitly requested
- no modern objects
- no photorealism
- no glossy 3D render look
- no anime influence
- no cinematic poster composition

Recommended generation target:

- landscape image
- final stored asset target: `640x400` PNG after selection/export

## Candidate 1: Observatory On The Headland

Primary request:

Storm-dark coastal observatory on a cliff above black surf, large brass dome, blind windows, sea spray, narrow headland path in the foreground, the building looming through rain and salt mist.

Scene/backdrop:

Rocky Atlantic-style headland at dusk or storm-day gloom, violent surf below, horizon mostly lost in weather.

Composition/framing:

Wide scene, observatory dominating the upper-middle frame, path leading the eye toward it.

Lighting/mood:

Low cold storm light with muted brass-green highlights and pale sea-foam accents.

Why test it:

- strongest overall match to the game premise
- best candidate for the startup screen if the silhouette reads well

## Candidate 2: Nearer Dome And Weathered Facade

Primary request:

Closer view of Asterfall Observatory with the brass dome, dark windows, wet stone, oxidized metal trim, and storm rain sheeting across the facade.

Scene/backdrop:

The upper station structure seen from the approach, with cliff air and rain but less emphasis on distant sea.

Composition/framing:

Medium-wide composition, architectural detail more prominent than in Candidate 1.

Lighting/mood:

Slate-grey weather with amber hints in metal reflections, severe and abandoned but not ruined.

Why test it:

- tests whether a closer architectural plate feels stronger than a wider landscape

## Candidate 3: Gate And Observatory Beyond

Primary request:

Chained iron gate in the foreground, rain-black bars and salt-furred lock, Asterfall Observatory looming beyond in storm haze with the dome visible above.

Scene/backdrop:

Approach to the station from outside the front gate, wet stone and ironwork carrying the foreground.

Composition/framing:

Foreground gate framing the observatory beyond, depth emphasized.

Lighting/mood:

Cold storm palette, iron blacks and blue-greys, restrained brass-green detail in the distant dome.

Why test it:

- tests whether a more “adventure-game screen” composition feels better than a pure exterior establishing shot

## Candidate 4: Observatory Above Violent Surf

Primary request:

A lonely cliffside observatory above violent black surf, jagged rocks below, spray blowing upward, dome and tower silhouetted against storm clouds.

Scene/backdrop:

The sea is more dominant here; the station feels isolated and endangered by weather.

Composition/framing:

Wide cinematic landscape, but still grounded as a game background plate rather than a poster.

Lighting/mood:

Dark blue-grey storm tones with narrow pale highlights in foam and wet stone.

Why test it:

- tests whether a more sea-dominant cover image better sells the setting

## Selection Criteria

Choose the candidate that best satisfies:

1. Reads immediately as a retro game background, not a modern concept painting.
2. Preserves a clear observatory silhouette and period feel.
3. Looks good in a medium-sized desktop panel.
4. Supports later room plates using the same style.
5. Feels atmospheric without becoming muddy or over-detailed.

## Next Step After Selection

Once one startup image is approved:

1. save it as `assets/images/startup/cover_v1.png`
2. use it as a style reference for the first room-image batch
3. begin with:
   - `cliff_path`
   - `front_gate`
   - `courtyard`
   - `foyer`
